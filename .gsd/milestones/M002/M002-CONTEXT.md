# M002: Advanced Systems & Economy Expansion

**Vision:** Building on the foundational multiplayer systems delivered in M001, M002 focuses on robust player economies, advanced progression (housing and advanced crafting), and PvP foundations.

## Potential Scopes
- **Player Housing System:** Instanced plots for players to build and decorate.
- **Advanced Crafting:** Tiered crafting progressions, specialization, and rare blueprint drops.
- **PvP Foundation:** Opt-in PvP zones, dueling, and structured arenas.
- **Mobile Port / Cross-Platform Polish:** Optimizing UI, inputs, and rendering for mobile devices.
