# Quest Design: Wounded Monkey (Pet Unlock)

**Date:** 2024-05-22
**Author:** Jules

## Overview
This quest chain introduces the concept of Pets to the player. It is a heartwarming mini-arc where the player rescues a wounded animal on the island, nursing it back to health. This aligns with the "Setting up Camp" and "First Pet Capture" roadmap items.

## Prerequisite
*   Completed Quest 1007 (Kindling).

## Quest Chain

### 1008. Strange Noises
*   **Start NPC:** Survivalist
*   **Description:** "I've been hearing strange noises coming from the bushes near the wreck. It sounds... distressed. Can you investigate? Be careful."
*   **Objectives:**
    *   `Talk` with `Survivalist` (to start).
    *   `Interact` with `RustlingBushes` (Location: Near Beach Wreck).
*   **Rewards:**
    *   EXP: 20
    *   Gold: 0

### 1009. A New Friend?
*   **Start NPC:** RustlingBushes / WoundedMonkey (Spawned after 1008 or just always there but interactable)
*   **Description:** "It's a small monkey! It looks hurt and scared. It needs medicine. The Survivalist mentioned seeing medicinal herbs inland."
*   **Objectives:**
    *   `Collect` x2 `MedicinalHerb` (Location: Jungle Edge).
*   **Rewards:**
    *   EXP: 50
    *   Gold: 0

### 1010. Nursing to Health
*   **Start NPC:** WoundedMonkey
*   **Description:** "You have the herbs. Apply them gently to the monkey's wounds."
*   **Objectives:**
    *   `Interact` with `WoundedMonkey`.
*   **Rewards:**
    *   EXP: 100
    *   PetUnlockId: 1 (Monkey)
    *   *System Note:* Unlocks the "Monkey" pet for the player.

## Technical Requirements
*   **New Reward Type:** `PetUnlockId` in `RewardDefinition`.
*   **Server Logic:** Handle `PetUnlockId` in `ClientSession.HandleClaimRewardAsync`.
*   **Data:** Add quests to `quests.json`.
