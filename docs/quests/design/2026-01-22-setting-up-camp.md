# Quest Design: Setting up Camp

## Overview
**Goal**: Establish the Beach Camp as a functional hub.
**Prerequisites**: "Improvised Defense" (1004) completed.
**Level Range**: 3-5.

## Quests

### 1005: Water Source
- **Start NPC**: Doctor (at Beach Camp).
- **Description**: The survivors are dehydrated. Find a source of fresh water inland.
- **Objectives**:
    - Collect 1 "FreshWater" (Interact with "Spring").
- **Rewards**: 50 Exp, 10 Gold.

### 1006: Fire Starter
- **Start NPC**: Doctor.
- **Description**: We need to boil the water. Ask the Survivalist for help starting a fire.
- **Objectives**:
    - Talk to "Survivalist".
- **Rewards**: 20 Exp, 0 Gold.

### 1007: Kindling
- **Start NPC**: Survivalist.
- **Description**: I can start a fire, but I need dry wood. The driftwood isn't enough.
- **Objectives**:
    - Collect 5 "DrySticks" (Interact with "DeadTree").
- **Rewards**: 100 Exp, 20 Gold, Item 101 (Potion x1).
