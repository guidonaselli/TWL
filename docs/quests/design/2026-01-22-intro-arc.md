# Design: Isla Brisa - Survivor's Arc (Act 1)

> **JULES-CONTEXT**: This is the main tutorial questline. All quest IDs, NPC names, item IDs,
> and objective types must match `quests.json` schema. When implementing, validate against
> `CONTENT_RULES.md` and `GAMEPLAY_CONTRACTS.md`. Rewards must be idempotent.

**Level Range:** 1-5
**Region:** Isla Brisa (Maps 0001-0005)
**Narrative Theme:** Survival, community building, first glimpse of Ancestral mystery.

---

## Narrative Summary

The player wakes on a beach after a violent storm destroyed their ship. Capitana Maren,
the ship's leader, organizes survivors into a functioning camp. Through a series of
escalating tasks, the player learns to gather, craft, fight, and eventually discovers
a cave with strange glowing crystals - the first hint that these islands hold ancient power.

**Unique Hook:** The player is not a "chosen one." They are one of many survivors.
Their distinction comes from being the first to venture beyond the camp and interact
with the Ancestral ruins. This earns them the informal title of "Explorador/a."

## Main Quest Chain: "Sobrevivir"

### Quest 1001: Despertar en la Playa
- **NPC:** Capitana Maren (Start + End)
- **Map:** 0001 (Playa del Naufragio)
- **Description:** "Everything hurts. The sun is blinding. Someone is shouting your name."
- **Objectives:**
  - Talk to Capitana Maren (Type: `Talk`, Target: `npc_maren`, Count: 1)
- **Rewards:** 10 EXP, 5 Gold
- **OnComplete:** Unlocks Quest 1002
- **Tutorial Teaches:** Movement, NPC interaction

### Quest 1002: Primer Sustento
- **Prerequisite:** Quest 1001
- **NPC:** Capitana Maren (Start) -> Nia (End)
- **Map:** 0001
- **Description:** "We need to eat. Nia says there are coconuts along the shore. Bring 3."
- **Objectives:**
  - Collect Coconuts (Type: `Collect`, Target: `item_coconut`, Count: 3)
- **Rewards:** 20 EXP, 1x Coconut Juice (consumable, heals 15 HP)
- **OnComplete:** Unlocks Quest 1003
- **Tutorial Teaches:** Item gathering, inventory

### Quest 1003: Reconocimiento
- **Prerequisite:** Quest 1002
- **NPC:** Nia (Start) -> Dr. Calloway (End)
- **Map:** 0001
- **Description:** "Good. Now check on Dr. Calloway - she's tending the wounded near the rocks."
- **Objectives:**
  - Talk to Dr. Calloway (Type: `Talk`, Target: `npc_calloway`, Count: 1)
- **Rewards:** 30 EXP, 2x Bandage (consumable, heals 25 HP)
- **OnComplete:** Unlocks Quest 1004
- **Tutorial Teaches:** Multiple NPCs, quest chains

### Quest 1004: Defensa Improvisada
- **Prerequisite:** Quest 1003
- **NPC:** Ruk (Start + End)
- **Map:** 0001
- **Description:** "The crabs are getting aggressive at night. Ruk can make a weapon if you bring driftwood."
- **Objectives:**
  - Collect Driftwood (Type: `Collect`, Target: `item_driftwood`, Count: 5)
- **Rewards:** 50 EXP, 1x Wooden Sword (ItemId: 103, equippable weapon, ATK +3)
- **OnComplete:** Unlocks Quest 1005, Quest 1008 (pet sidequest)
- **Tutorial Teaches:** Crafting concept, equipment
- **Flags:** `BindOnAcquire: true`, `UniquePerCharacter: true`

### Quest 1005: Fuego y Refugio
- **Prerequisite:** Quest 1004
- **NPC:** Nia (Start + End)
- **Map:** 0001 -> 0003
- **Description:** "We need a proper camp. Find a water source and kindling in the jungle edge."
- **Objectives:**
  - Find Fresh Water Source (Type: `Interact`, Target: `obj_stream`, Count: 1, Map: 0003)
  - Collect Dry Kindling (Type: `Collect`, Target: `item_kindling`, Count: 3, Map: 0003)
  - Collect Flint Stones (Type: `Collect`, Target: `item_flint`, Count: 2, Map: 0002)
- **Rewards:** 80 EXP, 15 Gold, Camp unlocked (narrative flag)
- **OnComplete:** Unlocks Quest 1006
- **Tutorial Teaches:** Multi-objective quests, zone exploration

### Quest 1006: Primera Sangre
- **Prerequisite:** Quest 1005
- **NPC:** Ruk (Start) -> Capitana Maren (End)
- **Map:** 0002 (Costa de Mareas)
- **Description:** "Time to test that sword. Clear the crabs from the tide pools so we can fish safely."
- **Objectives:**
  - Defeat Shore Crabs (Type: `Kill`, Target: `mob_shore_crab`, Count: 5)
- **Rewards:** 100 EXP, 20 Gold
- **OnComplete:** Unlocks Quest 1007, Combat Training sidequest (3001)
- **Tutorial Teaches:** Combat basics, turn-based battle

### Quest 1007: El Eco en la Cueva
- **Prerequisite:** Quest 1006
- **NPC:** El Viejo Coral (Start + End)
- **Map:** 0003 -> 0004 (Cueva del Eco)
- **Description:** "The old hermit says there's something in the cave that hums at night. Investigate."
- **Objectives:**
  - Enter Cueva del Eco (Type: `Reach`, Target: `map_0004`, Count: 1)
  - Examine the Stone Circle (Type: `Interact`, Target: `obj_stone_circle`, Count: 1)
  - Defeat Stone Guardian (Type: `Kill`, Target: `mob_stone_guardian`, Count: 1)
- **Rewards:** 150 EXP, 30 Gold, 1x Resonance Shard (key item, untradeable)
- **OnComplete:** Unlocks Quest 1018 (Travesia)
- **Tutorial Teaches:** Dungeon exploration, mini-boss combat, lore discovery
- **Narrative:** First encounter with Ancestral technology. The Stone Circle activates briefly,
  showing a holographic map of the archipelago. El Viejo Coral reveals he has been studying
  these ruins for years but never triggered a response before.

### Quest 1018: Travesia
- **Prerequisite:** Quest 1007, Level 5+
- **NPC:** Capitana Maren (Start + End)
- **Map:** 0001 -> 0005 (Mirador del Faro)
- **Description:** "We've spotted a city across the water. Time to repair a boat and make contact."
- **Objectives:**
  - Collect Sturdy Planks (Type: `Collect`, Target: `item_planks`, Count: 8)
  - Collect Sail Cloth (Type: `Collect`, Target: `item_sailcloth`, Count: 3)
  - Collect Vine Rope (Type: `Collect`, Target: `item_vine_rope`, Count: 5)
  - Build the Boat (Type: `Interact`, Target: `obj_boat_frame`, Count: 1, Map: 0001)
- **Rewards:** 200 EXP, 50 Gold, Unlocks Puerto Roca (Region 1)
- **OnComplete:** Player transitions to Puerto Roca. Isla Brisa remains accessible via boat.
- **Narrative:** Capitana Maren stays behind to manage the camp. She tasks the player with
  establishing contact with Puerto Roca and reporting back. This is the emotional farewell
  that marks the end of the tutorial and the beginning of the open game.
