# Design: Survivor's Beach Arc

**Date:** 2024-05-22
**Level Range:** 1-5
**Location:** Beach Start Zone

## Progression Goal
Introduce the player to the game loop: Accept Quest -> Do Objective -> Claim Reward.
Verify server-side validation of quest states.

## Quest Chain: "Survival Basics"

### Quest 1: Washed Ashore
*   **ID:** 1001
*   **NPC:** Captain (Start) -> Captain (End)
*   **Description:** "You just woke up on the beach. Your head hurts. The Captain is waving at you."
*   **Objectives:**
    *   Talk to Captain (Type: "Talk", Target: "Captain", Count: 1)
*   **Rewards:** 10 EXP, 5 Gold.
*   **OnComplete:** Unlocks Quest 1002.

### Quest 2: First Meal
*   **ID:** 1002
*   **Prerequisite:** Quest 1001 Completed.
*   **NPC:** Captain (Start) -> Quartermaster (End)
*   **Description:** "We need food. There are coconuts scattered around. Bring me 3."
*   **Objectives:**
    *   Collect Coconuts (Type: "Collect", Target: "Coconut", Count: 3)
*   **Rewards:** 20 EXP, Coconut Juice (Item).

### Quest 3: Reporting In
*   **ID:** 1003
*   **Prerequisite:** Quest 1002 Completed.
*   **NPC:** Quartermaster (Start) -> Doctor (End)
*   **Description:** "Good work. Now go check on the Doctor, she was checking the wounded."
*   **Objectives:**
    *   Talk to Doctor (Type: "Talk", Target: "Doctor", Count: 1)
*   **Rewards:** 30 EXP, Bandage (Item).
