# Hidden Ruins Arc (Jungle Fringe)

**Theme**: Exploration, Mystery, Ancient Civilization.
**Goal**: Gain access to the ancient ruins to uncover the secrets of the island.
**Level Range**: 15-20

## Main Quest Chain

### 1201: Strange Symbols
*   **Start**: Survivalist (Jungle Camp)
*   **Description**: "I found these strange markings on some debris near the cliff. Can you bring me a sample?"
*   **Objectives**:
    *   Collect "Strange Stone" (1) from "StrangeStoneDebris".
*   **Rewards**: 100 EXP, 10 Gold.

### 1202: The Door is Locked
*   **Start**: Survivalist
*   **Description**: "This stone matches the door to the ruins we found earlier. But it's locked. Maybe there's a clue on the nearby tablets."
*   **Prerequisites**: 1201
*   **Objectives**:
    *   Collect "Ancient Rubbing" (1) from "AncientTablet".
*   **Rewards**: 150 EXP, 20 Gold, Item: Ruins Key (8103).

### 1203: Guardians of the Past
*   **Start**: Auto (upon completing 1202) or Survivalist
*   **Description**: "The key opens the door, but a mechanical guardian has awakened!"
*   **Prerequisites**: 1202
*   **Objectives**:
    *   Kill "RuinsGuardian" (1).
*   **Rewards**: 300 EXP, 50 Gold.

### 1204: Inside the Ruins
*   **Start**: Survivalist
*   **Description**: "The guardian is down. Use the key to enter the ruins."
*   **Prerequisites**: 1203
*   **Objectives**:
    *   Interact with "RuinsEntrance".
*   **Rewards**: 200 EXP, Unlock: Ruins Hub.

## Sidequests

### 2201: Lost Explorer's Journal
*   **Start**: Found Item (Journal Page) or Doctor
*   **Description**: "These pages detail the flora of the jungle. Find the rest."
*   **Objectives**:
    *   Collect "Journal Page" (3) from "JournalPagePile".
*   **Rewards**: 150 EXP, Item: Explorer Hat (Cosmetic/Armor).

### 2202: Jungle Survival
*   **Start**: Doctor
*   **Description**: "The jungle is full of poison. We need to be prepared. Craft an Antidote."
*   **Objectives**:
    *   Craft "Antidote" (1) at "AlchemyTable".
*   **Rewards**: 100 EXP, Item: Antidote (3).
