# World Regions

## Puerto Roca
A bustling port city built on the ruins of an ancient colonial settlement. It serves as the main hub for adventurers arriving on the continent.

- **Theme:** Tropical, Colonial, Stone & Wood.
- **Key Locations:**
    - The Grand Plaza (Map 1000)
    - The Docks (Map 1001)
    - The Old Mines (Map 1003+)

## North Island
Wild and untamed, covered in dense jungle and ancient ruins.

## South Island
Volcanic terrain with dangerous flora and fauna.
