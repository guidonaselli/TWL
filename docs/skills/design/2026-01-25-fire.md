# Fire Tier 1 Design - 2026-01-25

## Purpose
Introduce Fire element Tier 1 skills to establish the aggressive, high-damage identity of the Fire element, while providing a basic support option that enhances offense.

## Skills

### 1. Flame Smash (Physical)
*   **ID:** 4001
*   **Description:** A basic fire physical attack.
*   **Target:** Single Enemy
*   **Scaling:** 1.2 * ATK
*   **Cost:** 5 SP
*   **Unlocks:** Level/Stats (Str 5)
*   **Evolution:** Rank 10 -> Stage 2 (or Next Skill).

### 2. Fireball (Magical)
*   **ID:** 4101
*   **Description:** A basic fire magic attack.
*   **Target:** Single Enemy
*   **Scaling:** 1.2 * MAT
*   **Cost:** 8 SP
*   **Unlocks:** Level/Stats (Int 5)

### 3. Fiery Will (Support)
*   **ID:** 4201
*   **Description:** Increases Attack power of an ally.
*   **Target:** Single Ally
*   **Effect:** BuffStats Atk +20
*   **Cost:** 15 SP
*   **Rationale:** Fire supports usually focus on offense enhancement rather than healing/defense.

## Power Budget & Balance
*   **Damage:** Standard T1 multipliers (1.2x) match Earth/Water.
*   **Support:** +20 Atk is significant at low levels (Base Str 20 -> Atk 40, +20 = 50% boost). Might be strong, but fits "Burst" identity.
*   **Counters:** Water element resists damage (0.5x). Cleanse can remove the Buff (if applied to enemy, but this is a buff). Dispel (Water T2) can remove this buff from enemies.

## Technical Implementation
*   Implemented `GetStatModifier` in `ServerCharacter` to enable `BuffStats` to actually affect derived stats.
*   Added JSON entries in `skills.json`.
*   Verified with `ServerFireSkillTests.cs`.
