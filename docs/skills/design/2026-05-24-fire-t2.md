# Fire T2 Skills Pack Design

## Overview
This pack introduces Tier 2 skills for the Fire element, focusing on "Burst Damage" and "Offensive Buffs" as per the Elemental Identity.

## Skills

### 4010: Blazing Strike (Physical)
- **Concept**: A powerful sweeping attack that hits a row of enemies with intense fire.
- **Element**: Fire
- **Branch**: Physical
- **Tier**: 2
- **Stage**: 1
- **Target**: RowEnemies
- **Cost**: 20 SP, 1 Cooldown
- **Scaling**: 1.5x Atk
- **Unlock**: ParentSkillId: 4003 (Flame Smash III), ParentSkillRank: 10
- **Effect**: Damage (Value 0, Tag Damage)

### 4110: Inferno (Magical)
- **Concept**: Engulfs the entire battlefield in flames.
- **Element**: Fire
- **Branch**: Magical
- **Tier**: 2
- **Stage**: 1
- **Target**: AllEnemies
- **Cost**: 35 SP, 2 Cooldown
- **Scaling**: 1.8x Mat
- **Unlock**: ParentSkillId: 4103 (Fireball III), ParentSkillRank: 10
- **Effect**: Damage (Value 0, Tag Damage)

### 4210: Inner Fire (Support)
- **Concept**: Stoked the magical potential of an ally, increasing their Magic Attack.
- **Element**: Fire
- **Branch**: Support
- **Tier**: 2
- **Stage**: 1
- **Target**: SingleAlly
- **Cost**: 25 SP, 2 Cooldown
- **Unlock**: ParentSkillId: 4203 (Fiery Will III), ParentSkillRank: 10
- **Effect**:
  - **Tag**: BuffStats
  - **Param**: Mat
  - **Value**: 25 (Base)
  - **Duration**: 3 Turns
  - **StackingPolicy**: RefreshDuration
  - **ConflictGroup**: Buff_Mat
  - **Priority**: 1
