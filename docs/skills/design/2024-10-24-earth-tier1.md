# Earth Tier 1 Design - 2024-10-24

## Purpose
Introduce the Earth element's foundation, focusing on physical durability and basic offense. Earth invariants: CON bias, Mitigation role.

## Skills

### 1. Rock Smash (Physical)
- **ID:** 60
- **Concept:** A heavy strike with a rock.
- **Target:** Single Enemy
- **Cost:** 8 SP
- **Scaling:**
  - ATK * 1.2
  - STR * 0.5
- **Effect:** Damage.
- **Unlock:** Level 1, STR 5.

### 2. Stone Bullet (Magical)
- **ID:** 61
- **Concept:** Shoots a small rock projectile.
- **Target:** Single Enemy
- **Cost:** 10 SP
- **Scaling:**
  - MAT * 1.2
- **Effect:** Damage.
- **Unlock:** Level 1, INT 5.

### 3. Earth Barrier (Support)
- **ID:** 62
- **Concept:** Surrounds an ally with floating stones, increasing defense.
- **Target:** Single Ally
- **Cost:** 15 SP
- **Scaling:**
  - WIS * 1.5 (Used for Buff Magnitude)
- **Effect:**
  - BuffStats ("Def")
  - Value: Dynamic (Wis * 1.5)
  - Duration: 3 turns.
- **Unlock:** Level 1, WIS 5.

## Balance
- **SP Costs:** Standard T1 (8-15 SP).
- **Scaling:** Standard T1 (1.2x).
- **Secondary Value:** Buff (Def) is valuable for Earth's tanky archetype.
- **Counters:**
  - Earth Barrier helps survive Physical burst.
  - Rock Smash deals physical damage (Earth > Water).
