# Water Tier 1 Skill Package Design
**Date:** 2024-10-24
**Element:** Water
**Focus:** Sustain, Basic Offense

## Overview
This package implements the foundational skills for the Water element, establishing its identity as the "Sustain/Support" element while providing necessary offensive tools for progression.

## Skills

### 1. Aqua Impact (Physical)
- **ID:** 3001
- **Concept:** A basic water-infused physical strike.
- **Branch:** Physical
- **Target:** Single Enemy
- **Cost:** 5 SP
- **Cooldown:** 0
- **Scaling:** 1.2 * ATK
- **Requirements:** STR 5
- **Unlock:** Level 1 + Stats
- **Evolution:** Rank 10 unlocks Tier 2 Physical.

### 2. Water Ball (Magical)
- **ID:** 3101
- **Concept:** A condensed sphere of water magic.
- **Branch:** Magical
- **Target:** Single Enemy
- **Cost:** 8 SP
- **Cooldown:** 0
- **Scaling:** 1.2 * MAT
- **Requirements:** INT 5
- **Unlock:** Level 1 + Stats
- **Evolution:** Rank 10 unlocks Tier 2 Magical.

### 3. Aqua Recover (Support)
- **ID:** 3201
- **Concept:** Restores health to an ally using water energy.
- **Branch:** Support
- **Target:** Single Ally
- **Cost:** 15 SP
- **Cooldown:** 0
- **Scaling:** 2.5 * WIS
- **Effect:** Heal (Restores HP)
- **Requirements:** WIS 5
- **Unlock:** Level 1 + Stats
- **Evolution:** Rank 10 unlocks Tier 2 Support (Purification).

## Balance Considerations
- **Aqua Recover:** Cost is higher (15 SP) to prevent infinite sustain in early game without investment in INT (MaxSP). Scaling is based on WIS to encourage Support builds.
- **Offense:** Standard coefficients (1.2) match Earth's `Rock Smash` and `Stone Bullet`, ensuring baseline parity.
- **Counters:** Water attacks will deal 1.5x damage to Fire enemies and 0.5x to Earth enemies.
