# 11-04-PLAN: Monster Expansion

## A) Goal
Expand `Content/Data/monsters.json` from 15 to 80+ entries, ensuring 10+ families and 4 elemental variants each, plus boss variants.

## B) Context / Traceability
- **Requirement**: CONT-02
- **Why**: A robust spawn table requires varied monster pools. The WLO-inspired elemental cycle (Water > Fire > Wind > Earth) mandates that every family possesses multiple variants to force tactical party adaptation.
- **Dependencies**: None.

## C) Step-by-Step Implementation Instructions
1. **Open the File**: Modify `Content/Data/monsters.json`.
2. **Implement Monster Families**:
   - Expand the existing 15 monsters to reach 80+.
   - Add new families (e.g., Slime, Goblin, Skeleton, Golem, Dragon, Elemental, Beast, Plant, Insect, Construct).
3. **Implement Elemental Variants**:
   - For each family, add Earth, Water, Fire, and Wind variants.
   - Differentiate variants through specific stats or skill assignments.
4. **Implement Boss Variants**:
   - Add 8 distinct bosses, one tailored for each region (Levels 15, 30, 45, 60, 75, 85, 95, 100).
   - Ensure boss variants have high HP, elevated resistances, and unique multi-target skills.
5. **ID Rules**:
   - All Monster IDs must be 1000 or greater.
   - Ensure NO duplicate IDs.

## D) Testing Requirements
- **Validation**: Ensure `monsters.json` is valid JSON.
- **Test Suite**: Run `ContentValidationTests` to verify no duplicate IDs and that referenced Skills exist.
